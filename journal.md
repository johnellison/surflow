### 2026-06-15 · Klotok's Right (Klungkung)
**Window:** 06:00 – 07:50 · at peak by 06:15
**Conditions:** ~1.7m @ 11s SSW, tide rising to ~2.2m by exit · onshore morning trades
**Wave count:** 1 ridden · 3+ missed
**Highlights:** Caught a bomb — solid 6–7ft overhead, steep drop. Got in clean.
**Working on:** Peak positioning · paddle commitment
**Rating:** 3/5
**Notes:**
- Entry easy and clean. One other surfer sitting well south of the peak — paddled around him, sat on the peak.
- **Miss #1:** Too far inside/deep, not close enough to the peak, underpaddled. Missed it.
- **Wave caught:** Second wave, a bomb. Closed out immediately — dropped too deep into a steep face and the wave started collapsing as I was still on the drop. No bottom turn. Just the drop and then whitewash.
- Paddled for a couple more, missed — swell went flat after that.
- **Exit at 07:45 — exactly as Julien warned.** Tide ~2.2m, shore break heavy. Big set came through during exit. Had to scramble around the impact zone, multiple duck dives to avoid getting pushed onto the rocks. Made it out unscathed.
- One wave only but it was solid. Session was short by necessity — swell died and the exit window forced the call.

**What to take forward:** Sit closer to the peak. On a closeout bomb, don't drop too deep into the face — if you're going to get one ride out of it, commit to the shoulder earlier. Exit before 07:45 on a tide like this.
